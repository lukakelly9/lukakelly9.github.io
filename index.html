About Me

I'm a passionate data scientist and technology enthusiast with expertise in Python, SQL, data visualization, and machine learning. My journey in the world of data has been marked by a commitment to innovation and a drive to solve complex problems.

With a Master's in Management and Organizational Leadership from Penn State, a Certificate in Data Science Immersive (DSIR) from General Assembly, and a B.S. in Psychology and Neuroscience, I bring a unique blend of business acumen, data science skills, and an understanding of the human mind to the table.

I'm dedicated to harnessing the power of data to drive business success and make informed decisions. What truly sets my heart on fire, though, is my aspiration to launch a career in neurotechnology, specifically in Brain Computer Interfaces (BCIs). I'm on a mission to collaborate with an innovative team that shares my passion for unlocking the potential of the human brain when combined with technology.

What sets me apart is my ability to translate complex data into actionable insights. I thrive on tackling challenges and finding creative solutions to drive business impact. My ultimate goal is to help lead the charge in creating a world where neurotechnology can restore capabilities such as vision, motor function, and speech.

I'm always open to connecting with fellow data enthusiasts, business leaders, and professionals who share my vision for the future of neurotechnology. Let's collaborate and explore the endless possibilities that data and technology have to offer in the realm of neuroscience and human potential.
